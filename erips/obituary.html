Vexilia Blessed-New-Dawn - An Obituary

Beloved Inventor and Friend to the Downtrodden ─── An obituary by Celiza Legs-Up-High

Vexilia, technically, isn't dead. Her body still walks Spire, though controlled by a higher power. But the friend and confidant that I've known for decades is gone. Not to speak ill of the Gods, but... I miss her. She was a loyal friend, a champion of the underclasses, and one of the kindest masters to her drow servants that I've ever met. I hope that your soul may rest when Brother Harvest departs this world. Vexilia. My dear friend and mentor. We will miss you. Goodnight. Signed, your friend, Celiza Legs-Up-High.
